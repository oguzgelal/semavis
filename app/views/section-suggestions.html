Analyzing the Heatmap

The goal is to get the shaded areas (clusters) and other dense regions closer to eachother as much as possible. So first chose a source cluster / region, and move it closer to a destination cluster.

  • Step 1 - Click on the source cluster / region
  • Step 2 - Click on the destination cluster
  • Step 3 - Click on the map to reset
